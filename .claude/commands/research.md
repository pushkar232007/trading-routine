---
description: Research current holdings, watchlist, and macro catalysts using native web search
---

Read `memory/strategy.md` and `memory/portfolio.md` first so you know what you currently hold and
what you're watching.

Then, using WebSearch and WebFetch (no Perplexity in this setup):

1. For each open position in `memory/portfolio.md`, check for material news in the last 24-48
   hours (earnings, guidance changes, analyst actions, sector news).
2. For each ticker on the watchlist in `memory/strategy.md`, check for new catalysts that would
   justify opening a position.
3. Check the macro calendar for today/this week (Fed events, CPI, jobs report, major earnings).
4. Form a short list of high-conviction ideas, each with: ticker, thesis in one sentence, and
   why now.

Append findings to `memory/research-log.md` in the documented format — terse, ticker-first, one
or two lines per source. Do not write a long narrative; future routines need to skim this fast.

Do not place any trades from this command — that's the job of `/trade`.
